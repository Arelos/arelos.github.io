About Arelos

Arelos is a new and upcoming linux distribution that aims to offer a fast, free, beautiful and no frills experience for the desktop, with those awesome OS X looks.

We are a small team of developers, designers and linux enthusiasts working our backs into Arelos; and we hope you'll love it the first time you power your PC on.
